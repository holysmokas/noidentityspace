Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to NoIdentity.Space. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Comments: If we add a commenting system in the future
  • Contact Information: When you reach out to us via email

Automatically Collected Information

Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Referring websites
  • Device type and browser
  • General location (country/city level only)

Cookies: We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics tracking
  • Google AdSense advertising
  • Remembering your preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Send you our newsletter (only if you subscribe)
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Understand which topics interest our readers
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and process data. Google's use of data is governed by their Privacy Policy. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the browser add-on.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we process. We use HTTPS encryption to secure data transmission.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR or CCPA, including:

  • Right of access and port data
  • Right to rectification and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, contact us:

Email: privacy@noidentity.space
Website: https://noidentity.space/contact

Commitment to Privacy

At NoIdentity.Space, we believe in practicing what we preach. As a website focused on digital privacy and anonymity, we are committed to:

  • Collecting minimal data necessary
  • Being transparent about our data practices
  • Never selling your personal information
  • Protecting your privacy to the best of our ability
  • Continuously improving our privacy practices

This privacy policy is provided as a template and should be reviewed by a legal professional before use.
